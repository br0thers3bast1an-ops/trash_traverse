▸ THE MISSION

The waste heaps are overflowing. Navigate scorching lava lanes and moving cargo trains to collect toxic trash and deliver it to the incinerator above. Clear all waste before your lives run out — and don't touch the lava.

  • ▬Moving trains — ride them safely across lava lanes
  • ~Lava rivers — instant death if touched without a platform
  • ↑Incinerator — reach the top while carrying trash to score
